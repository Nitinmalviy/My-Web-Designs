Fragrance

Perfumes are classified into 5 main groups loosely based on their concentration of aromatic compounds.

Eau de toilette has a low concentration of essential oils and a high concentration of alcohol. It dissipates quickly and lasts 2 to 3 hours. It is a cheaper option and often used for daywear.
